Get to Know Pollimation!

Pollimation is your one-stop-shop for all your animation needs! We believe that everyone should be able to make their ideas come true, and we want to help you and your project. That's why we work tirelessly to bring you the visualizations you require. Whether you need a hand-drawn character animated for your next short or some stylish 3D product animation, Pollimation is right for you. Our team of talented animators will work closely with your group to ensure your vision gets brought to life.

  • Able to record and clean up mocap performances
  • Modelling and rigging services also available
